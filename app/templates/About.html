How It Works

Create a pet profile

To create a pet profile, simply take a picture of your pet. The app will automatically recognize the basic information of your pet.

Talk to our AI assistant

Text to our AI assistant about your pet's problem. It will give you initial suggestions and tell your which clinic or department fit that symptom the best.

Find matched veterinarian

The app will then suggest a list of veterinarians that have specialty in that department, you can check their profiles and pick one that you prefer.

Book a session

You can communicate with the vet you choose by video calling in the app, and book an in-person session if necessary.
